FAQ

How do I play Last Man Standing?
Pick one team each round. If your team wins, you continue. If your team draws or loses, you're eliminated. You cannot reuse the same team later.
Is it free to join?
Yes. Many weekly events as well as the $100,000 World Cup tournament are 100% Free to Play.
What do I win?
There are weekly prizes and a much larger World Cup jackpot for the final survivor. These prizes range from $1,000 to $100,000
